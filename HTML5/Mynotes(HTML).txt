HTML stands for Hyper Text Mark up Language

Structure of HTML:
<!DOCTYPE html>
<html>
    <head><title>Structure of html</title></head>
    <body>
        <h3><b>Hello Html</b></h3>
    </body>
</html>

Headings:
There are 6 heading tags numbered from <h1> to <h6>
Which varies in size based on the priority of the Heading of the Content
